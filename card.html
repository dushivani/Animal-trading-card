Parrot

Parrots are believed to be one of the most intelligent bird species. There are around 372 different parrot species.

  • Scientific Name: Psittaciformes
  • Average Length: 100 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Tropical continents

Parrots have curved bills (beaks), strong legs and clawed feet. Parrots are often brightly coloured. There are around 372 different parrot species. Parrots are the only birds that can eat with their feet. Some parrots can live for over 80 years. Parrots have strong beaks. Many parrots mate for life.
